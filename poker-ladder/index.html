How it works

✕

End goal: climb all 21 tables, from Newbie Corner to Oligarch, using only money won at the tables. Everyone starts at the bottom. First to the top wins.

Rules

  • You play the tables in order, one at a time.
  • No skipping. Games at higher tables don't count, even if you have enough to buy in.
  • You can only buy in with what's in your ladder bankroll. This is tracked automatically; a buy-in bigger than your bankroll simply won't be counted.
  • Within a table, buy in with anything from its minimum to its maximum.
  • You can keep playing your usual poker games alongside the ladder. The system works out which games are part of the climb and ignores the rest.
  • Taking an extended break? Hit Pause ladder on your profile and nothing is tracked until you resume.

How it works

  1. Start at Newbie Corner: play poker as you normally would, bank as much as you can, cash out.
  2. With enough for the next table's minimum, take a seat there. The climb registers when you sit down. Continue in this fashion.
  3. Left a table with $0? If your bankroll still covers a buy-in there, sit back down; the buy-in comes out of your tracked bankroll.
  4. If you can no longer afford your table, you drop to the highest table your bankroll covers.
  5. Cash out with less than $500 in your bankroll (the lowest table's minimum) and that's a bust: this cycle of the ladder is over and you start again from the bottom.
  6. Do as many cycles as you wish. Sign in and press Sync to update your climb.

Your climb starts the moment you sign up, so old poker history doesn't count. The app reads only your poker join/leave log entries. Nothing is stored except your ladder progress, and you can delete it anytime from your profile.

Help

✕
My game isn't syncing but I just played

The two usual reasons a game doesn't count:

  • You skipped ahead: only your current table and the next one up are part of the ladder.
  • You bought in with more than your ladder bankroll: buy-ins must fit what you've won at the tables.
Nothing is updating at all

It might be an API issue on Torn's side. Wait a minute and press Sync again. While Torn's API is down, the ladder can't read anything.

If the issue persists, send a message to Systoned.
