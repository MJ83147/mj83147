How it works

✕
  1. The goal is to get through all 21 tables, from Newbie Corner to Oligarch. Everyone starts at the bottom table.
  2. Play Torn poker normally. When you sign in, the app reads only your poker join and leave log entries and tracks how far you have climbed.
  3. You move up one table at a time: cash out with enough bankroll to cover the next table's minimum, then take a seat there. The climb registers when you sit down, and winning a huge pot doesn't let you skip tables.
  4. Only sessions at your table or the next one up count, and only with money you've won at the tables (no topping up from outside). Anything else is off-ladder fun and is ignored.
  5. If you cash out with less than your table's minimum, you drop to the highest table you can still afford and it counts as a bust. Below Newbie Corner's minimum your cycle ends; your next buy-in at the bottom starts a fresh one. First to reach Oligarch tops the ladder.

Your climb starts the moment you sign up, so old poker history doesn't count. Nothing is stored except your ladder progress, and you can delete it anytime from your profile.
