How it works

✕
  1. The goal is to get through all 21 tables, from Newbie Corner to Oligarch. Everyone starts at the bottom table with whatever they buy in with — there is no handed-out stake.
  2. Play Torn poker normally. When you sign in, the app reads only your poker join and leave log entries and tracks how far you have climbed.
  3. You move up one table at a time. To advance you must have won enough at your table to cover the next table's minimum buy-in, then go sit at it. You can't skip tables, and winning a huge pot at a low table doesn't jump you ahead.
  4. You can only buy in with money you've won at the tables — no topping up from outside. A bigger stake just means you can rebuy after a bad hand, as long as you can still cover the buy-in.
  5. If you leave a table with less than its minimum, you drop down to the highest table your remaining money can still buy into (all the way to Newbie Corner if you've lost it) and it counts as a bust. First to reach Oligarch tops the ladder.

Your climb starts the moment you sign up, so old poker history doesn't count. Nothing is stored except your ladder progress, and you can delete it anytime from your profile.
