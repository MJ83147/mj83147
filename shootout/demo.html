Tournament Rules

Format. Progressive Shootout, Texas Hold'em.

Stage 1. 9-player tables. Top 3 advance: 1st → Stage 4, 2nd → Stage 3, 3rd → Stage 2.

Stage 2 + 3. 6-player tables. Winner only advances.

Stage 4. 9-player tables, top 3 advance. Plays in sub-rounds (4a, 4b, 4c…) until ≤9 players remain — that's the Final Table.

Rebuys. $25M after Stage 1, $50M after Stage 2, $75M after Stage 3. Max 3 per player. No rebuys after Stage 4 begins.
