Player messages

Ready-made messages to send players after a result. Pick the stage, then click to copy the message that fits their finish.

▾ Steward availability

Click a slot to toggle whether the steward can cover it. Slots without tables yet are dimmed but still pickable so stewards can plan ahead. Removing a slot will auto-unassign the steward from any table at that slot.

▾ Tables by time slot

Tables appear here once they've been generated. Use the tabs to switch between stages (Stage 1, its rebuy, Stage 2, and so on); within a stage they're grouped by time slot. Assign one steward per table from those who marked this slot available, or click Auto-assign stewards to fill unassigned tables in one go (existing assignments are kept). The Copy button puts the table's Torn IDs on your clipboard as a comma-separated list, ready to paste into Torn's poker invite.
