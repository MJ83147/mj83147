Stewards

Managed in the Stewards tab. Each signs in with their Torn ID.

Steward availability

Click a slot to toggle whether the steward can cover it. Slots without tables yet are dimmed but still pickable so stewards can plan ahead. Removing a slot will auto-unassign the steward from any table at that slot.

Tables by time slot

Assign one steward per table from those who marked this slot available. The Copy button puts the table's Torn IDs on your clipboard as a comma-separated list, ready to paste into Torn's poker invite.
